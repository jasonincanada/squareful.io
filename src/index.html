How to Play

Each board is a 36x36 square tiled with smaller squares. Every board has one 1x1 tile, two 2x2 tiles, and so on up to eight 8x8 tiles.

  • Click Random Puzzle to pick a random puzzle board.
  • Fill in the missing squares!
  • Use the mouse wheel to change the square size.
  • Left-click to place the square.
